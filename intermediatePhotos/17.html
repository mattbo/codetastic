Some Rules of Thumb

  • Shoot at sunrise or sunset when possible -- the long shadows make details 'pop'
  • No exposure longer than half the length of your lens (for SLRs -- if you have a 75mm lens, don't go longer than 1/75th second)
  • Lower your ISO! It's very easy to forget and leave it at 800 from the night before
  • Use flickr to learn -- all the photos have details about the settings used to take them!
  • Take lots of pictures!!!
