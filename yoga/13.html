Aperture

  • Also called F-stop
  • How big is the opening allowing in light
  • Counter intuitive numbering
    • Large F-stop number (i.e. 22 or 24) is a small opening
    • Small F-stop number (i.e. 2.0 or 3.5) is a large opening
  • Large F-stop, large depth of field, long exposure
  • Small F-stop, shallow depth of field, short exposure
